How GRIEEVIO Works in 4 Steps
01

Report

Type or speak your complaint in any language. Attach a photo and pin the exact location on the map.

02

AI Routes

Our AI reads the complaint, assigns a category, sets priority, and routes it to the right department automatically.

03

Admin Acts

Municipal admins review, dispatch field teams, update status in real-time, and upload resolution evidence.

You're Notified
